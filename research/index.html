• My research examines memory within an architectural framework.

    Drawing upon influences from literature (Proust), philosophy (Benjamin; Cicero), cognitive science (Atkinson, Neath, Shiffrin) and the media arts (Boltanski, Chicago, Emin), my aim is to investigate memory as an active, fluid and pervasive process using postmodern ideologies.
